What Is PogoDev?

PogoDev is an unofficial PokemonGO development community born out of the sub-reddit /r/pokemongodev and run by keyphact and the PogoDev team.

Can I Join PogoDev?

Sure, just find us via any one of our channels and participate.

What is API Hashing Service by PF and how do I use it

The API Hashing Service by PF is a service to generate valid request hashes to work with the this unofficial PokemonGO API. All informatio you need to get started is here: https://talk.pogodev.org/d/54-getting-started-with-our-api-hashing-service.

I was banned from PogoDev for XXX reason, why?

Our rules are quite clear in stating what we do and do not allow in this community, you can read them here.

My post / comment / link was removed from PogoDev for XXX reason, why?

Again, our rules are quite clear in stating what we do and do not allow in this community, you can read them here.

How do I report abuse or someone breaking the rules?

We take abuse and rule-breaking very seriously here, as we want to create a community where all feel safe and welcomed. If you feel that there is a person or persons that are disrupting this, please drop by our #abuse channel in Discord and report your case.

How can I share my project / application / website?

You can make a post on our releases sub-forum here: https://talk.pogodev.org/t/releases.

My account / scanner / application has been banned from PokemonGO, please help?

Please refer to our forum post in regards to this: https://talk.pogodev.org/d/46-faq-have-i-been-banned-from-pokemongo.
